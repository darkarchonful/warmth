## Description  (max 4000 chars)

Warmth is a quiet little app for two people who want to spend better time
together.

It's not a feed, not a social network, and not for meeting strangers. It's just
for you and your partner — a shared space to find things to do, decide together,
and remember the moments that mattered.

**How it works**

• Swipe through a deck of date ideas and activities — from a five-minute morning
  hug to a weekend away.
• When you both like the same idea, it becomes a match and lands in your shared
  Plans.
• Agree on a time, do the thing, then save it as a Memory — with your own
  ratings and notes.
• Leave little messages for each other in any plan or memory.
• Loved something? Ask to do it again with one tap.

**Why couples like it**

• You decide together. Nothing gets added to your plans unless you both say yes.
• Gentle nudges, never spam. A small reminder if a plan you both agreed on is
  going stale — written for the two of you, not a generic ping.
• Ideas that fit the season, so suggestions feel right for the time of year.
• Write your own cards when the app's ideas aren't quite "you."
• Private by design. Your space is just the two of you — no followers, no likes
  from outside, no ads, no tracking.

**Made for real life**

Warmth works best as a habit — a place you open together on a slow evening or a
free weekend to answer the simple question, "what should we do?" Some ideas take
five minutes, some take a whole trip. The point isn't the app. The point is the
time you spend because of it.

Pair with your partner using a simple invite code, and you're set.

—

Warmth respects your privacy. We collect only what the app needs to work, we
never sell your data, and we don't use advertising or third-party tracking.
