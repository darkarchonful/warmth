# Warmth — Midjourney Prompts for Activity Cards

## Style Guide (use as base for every prompt)

**Style suffix to append to all prompts:**
```
watercolor and pencil sketch, two anonymous human silhouettes, soft warm peach and cream washes, dusty rose accents, minimal ink linework, hand-drawn intimate feel, no faces, no detail on figures, soft blurred background, plenty of negative space, --ar 4:3 --style raw --v 6
```

**Process:**
1. Generate card 1 with full prompt + style suffix.
2. Lock the seed (`--seed XXXX`) once you find the right look.
3. Reuse `--seed` for all subsequent cards to keep style consistent.
4. Upscale only the keepers; iterate on the rest.
