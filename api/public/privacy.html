Warmth — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

Warmth is an app for couples to discover and plan things to do together. We collect only what the app needs to work, we never sell your data, and we don't use advertising or third-party tracking. This policy explains what we store, why, and the control you have over it.

Who we are

Warmth is operated by the developer of dbtvault-solutions.tech. For any privacy question or request, contact darkarchonful@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Account details — your email address, the display name you choose, and (optionally) an avatar image and gender. These come from your sign-in method or what you enter.
  • Sign-in identifiers — if you use Sign in with Google or Sign in with Apple, we store the identifier those services give us so we can recognise you on your next visit. If you use email sign-in, we send a one-time code to your email; the code is short-lived and deleted after use.
  • Your couple activity — who you are paired with, the activities you swipe on, the plans you agree to, the memories you save (including any ratings and notes you write), and messages you post in a plan's or memory's comment thread. This content is shared with your paired partner — that is the point of the app.
  • Notifications — if you allow push notifications, we store a device push token so we can send reminders and nudges. You can turn this off in your device settings at any time.
  • Technical basics — your time zone (so reminders arrive at a sensible hour) and a last-login timestamp.

What we do NOT collect

  • No precise or GPS location — we only use the coarse time zone your device reports.
  • No advertising identifiers, ad networks, or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs.
  • No payment or financial information is collected by the app itself; any future purchases are handled by Apple, and we never see your card details.

How we use your information

  • To create your account and pair you with your partner.
  • To show your shared deck, plans, memories, and comments to you and your partner.
  • To send the reminders and nudges you opt into.
  • To keep the service secure and working (e.g. signing you in, preventing abuse).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone for marketing, and we do not use it to build advertising profiles.

Service providers

A small number of trusted providers process data strictly on our behalf to make the app function:

  • Google and Apple — only if you choose their sign-in option, to verify your identity.
  • Resend — to deliver the one-time sign-in code to your email address.
  • Expo / Apple Push Notification service / Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — to deliver push notifications to your device, if enabled.

Your account and couple data is stored on servers we operate ourselves; it is not handed to a third-party data broker.

Data retention

  • We keep your account and content while your account is active.
  • When you unpair from a partner, the shared content for that pairing (swipes, plans, and memories) is deleted.
  • When you delete your account from within the app, we remove your personal data. Anonymised, non-identifying records may remain only where needed for the integrity of the service.
  • One-time email sign-in codes are deleted shortly after they are used or expire.

Your rights and choices

  • Delete your account — available directly in the app under Settings. This removes your personal data.
  • Unpair — end a pairing at any time; shared content for that pairing is wiped.
  • Notifications — turn push notifications on or off in your device settings.
  • Access or correction — contact us at the email below to request a copy of your data or to correct it.

Children

Warmth is intended for adults. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age required in their country to consent to online services.

Security

Connections to Warmth are encrypted in transit (HTTPS). We restrict access to stored data and take reasonable measures to protect it, though no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

Contact

Questions or requests: darkarchonful@gmail.com
