Frequently asked

How do I pair with my partner?

One of you taps Create Invite and shares the code; the other enters it under Join with a code. Once joined, you'll both land on your shared deck.

How does the deck work?

Swipe through ideas — right to like, left to pass. When you both like the same idea, it becomes a shared Plan. Do it together, then save it as a Memory with your own ratings and notes.

Why can't I swipe more cards?

To keep things intentional, Warmth asks you to wrap up your current plans before piling on new ones. Complete or clear a plan and the deck opens back up.

How do I unpair?

Open Settings (from the top of your deck) and choose to unpair. This ends the pairing for both of you and clears the shared plans, swipes, and memories.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings and choose Delete account. This permanently removes your account and personal data.

I'm not getting notifications.

Make sure notifications are allowed for Warmth in your device Settings. You can turn them off there any time, too.
